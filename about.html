My top two sports are football and wrestling as I love watching and competing in them, I love hunting deer, and my favorite movies are thrillers. I am including a picture of what trapshooting (one of many shooting sports events) so you can get an idea of what it loolks like!

My favorite hobby is probably shooting sports, and specifcally trapshooting within that. To briefly describe it, there are five stations about 15 yards from where a bunker is. In the bunker is a machine that throws clay targets and these targets are thrown in a 135 degree range. At each of the 5 stations, you shoot at 5 targets and then rotate. You do this until all 5 stations are completed, for a total of 25 targets. Most events will have 4 rounds for a grand total of 100 targets.
